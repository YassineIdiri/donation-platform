Your privacy is critically important to us. At [Association Name], we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law or process your donations.
  • We store personal information only as long as we have a reason to keep it.

1 Data Collection

When you make a donation, we collect the following information to process the transaction and issue your tax receipt:

  • Identity: First name, Last name, Email address.
  • Payment Data: Handled securely by our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers.

2 Usage of Data

Receipt Issuance

Generating and sending your official tax receipt (Cerfa).

Communication

Sending a confirmation email and, only if you consented, our newsletter.

3 Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. To exercise these rights, simply contact us at contact@association.org.

4 Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the functioning of the donation form (session security, CSRF protection). We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.
